## 2. Live teardown: Peec AI (batch 1)

Positioning: "AI search analytics for marketing teams." Track/analyze/improve via Visibility, Position, Sentiment. 2,000+ teams, 4.9/5 G2, "recommended on Reddit."

Pricing (observed on pricing page):
- Starter $95/mo: 50 prompts, choose 3 models, unlimited users, daily tracking, 1 project, 1 country, MCP
- Pro $245/mo (most popular): 150 prompts, 2 projects, 3 countries
- Advanced $495/mo: 350 prompts, 5 projects, Looker Studio, API
- Enterprise custom: up to 11 LLM models, SSO, unlimited projects/countries
- Models: ChatGPT, AI Mode, AI Overviews, Copilot, Perplexity, Gemini (+ Claude/GPT-4-Search/Deepseek/Qwen/Mistral enterprise-only)

Signature moves worth copying:
- UNLIMITED USER SEATS on every plan (removes per-seat friction for teams/agencies)
- MCP integration marketed as a feature (connect Peec to Claude/Cursor/n8n) — positions as platform
- Free per-engine visibility trackers (ChatGPT / Gemini / AI Mode) as separate SEO-landing lead magnets
- Industry benchmarks + GEO email course + free GEO guide as education funnel
- Public ARR milestone disclosure = recurring press loop
- Work-email-gated signup (Google/Microsoft/SSO/magic link), no credit card

Weaknesses/openings:
- $95 floor prices out solopreneurs/small agencies; real all-in cost at Pro ~€217-249 with add-ons
- Monitoring-first: light on "now fix it" execution
- Country/project limits punish agencies with many small clients

## 5. Success patterns (cross-tool synthesis)

Onboarding that converts:
1. Instant value BEFORE signup: free "enter domain -> AI-visibility score in 60s, no signup" (category-standard lead magnet)
2. Run analysis on the user's own domain DURING onboarding (SEObot/SEOJuice "enter URL and press go")
3. No-credit-card trials beat demo-gating for self-serve
4. Agency-vs-Brand segmentation question first (Otterly)
5. Auto-generate suggested prompts from the domain (removes the "what do I track?" cold start)

Pricing mechanics that win the value-seeker market:
- FLAT beats credits at entry (credit exhaustion is the #1 complaint against Rankscale/Semdash)
- ALL ENGINES INCLUDED, zero add-ons (inverts the loudest Otterly/Peec grievance)
- Unlimited seats (Peec's signature move)
- Free-forever tier as funnel (Trakkr), optionally free-with-backlink (SEOJuice link loop)
- Sweet spot: $29-49/mo flat, generous prompts, all engines, no CC trial

Growth loops ranked for a solo founder:
1. Free no-signup checker -> email -> paid monitoring
2. Programmatic "X vs Y" comparison pages (they also get cited by the AI engines themselves — meta-loop)
3. Free-tier-with-backlink virality
4. Public milestone/ARR disclosure (once real)
5. AppSumo LTD for review volume injection
6. Founder-led onboarding calls (retention + reviews)

Shareable "screenshot moments" that drive word of mouth:
- Share-of-voice vs named competitors ("they own 'best alternative to' answers; you're invisible")
- Hallucination detection ("AI tells buyers false things about you")
- "Which domains get cited instead of you" (Reddit/G2 dominance reveal)

## 6. The open seats (where PresenceOS wins)

## 7. Live teardown: Otterly.AI IN-PRODUCT (batch 2, agency path)

Screenshots: `docs/market-intelligence/screenshots/` (page-2026-07-16T19-48* through 20-08*, plus otterly-01..07 for the funnel).

Onboarding sequence (4 steps, ~3 min to first report):
1. About You — first/last name, Agency vs Brand selector, "how did you hear about us"
2. Brand Info — website, brand name, country, language (all required)
3. Competitors — AUTO-SUGGESTED competitor list from domain analysis, editable, with a live Brand Ranking preview table beside it. (Accuracy miss observed: suggested Canadian food brands for an AI consulting domain — their fallback is weak. Opportunity: do this well.)
4. Prompts — AI-GENERATED 15 industry-specific prompts from the domain (correctly inferred "AI consulting" from mytegroup.com). All pre-selected, custom prompts addable, nudge to "add at least 15 prompts for better data." This domain-to-prompt pipeline is their core onboarding magic and the thing to replicate.
Then: "Checking everything..." processing screen -> "Brand Reports — Explained" education modal -> "Your brand report is ready!"

Information architecture (sidebar):
- BRAND REPORT: brand selector / Overview / Prompts / Citations / Recommendations / Agents analytics
- GENERAL: AI Prompt Research / Search Prompts / GEO Audit Tools / Resources
- ADMIN: Workspaces / Team Management / API Keys / Billing

Overview dashboard: date-range + tags + engine + country filters; Brand Mentions, Average Brand Position, Brand Coverage Over Time chart, Domain Citation, Citations Share, top-3 cited URLs; Brand Ranking table (brand vs competitors: Sentiment, Mentions, Brand Coverage %, Share of Voice); Top Prompts by Brand Mentions.

Prompts table columns: prompt, brand coverage %, sentiment, intent volume, brand mentions, total brand mentions, domain citations, total domain citations, competitors, tags. CSV export. Notably: total-domain-citations column shows real competitive-landscape numbers even for a brand with zero mentions — the empty state still shows the competitive world, which softens the cold start.

Copy-worthy: (1) domain->industry->prompts generation; (2) agency multi-brand workspaces as first onboarding question; (3) competitor auto-suggestion with ranking preview; (4) education modal before first report; (5) prompt-centric IA.

## 15. FINAL SYNTHESIS — the winning formula (all 9 teardowns + research)

Every winner does 4 things; nobody does all 6 below. PresenceOS can:

1. INSTANT VALUE BEFORE PAYMENT (Trakkr's pre-paywall competitor reveal, Foglift's no-signup audit, HubSpot grader) — vs Rankscale's fatal paywall-first.
2. TRANSPARENT GENEROUS PRICING (Foglift's per-engine token table + calculator; LLMrefs' unlimited-domains flat $79; Peec's unlimited seats; Trakkr's "all models included") — vs opaque credits and per-engine add-ons, the two loudest complaints.
3. ALL ENGINES INCLUDED at every tier (Rankscale's "No Upsell" message, Trakkr's 8 models) — inverts Otterly's $9 add-ons and Peec's "choose 3 models."
4. ACTIONS, NOT JUST DASHBOARDS (Trakkr's weekly playbooks + MCP execution; Athena's content agents; Foglift's copy-paste code fixes; OTTO's auto-deploy) — kills the monitoring-only churn trap.
5. ATTRIBUTION (Trakkr GA4 2-min setup; Athena GA4/GSC/Shopify) — answers "is this making money?"
6. PROOF (nobody: no competitor ships multi-run confidence intervals + tamper-evident evidence; Mangools' "5x per prompt" and LLMrefs' "statistical significance" messaging prove accuracy SELLS) — PresenceOS's unique weapon, already built in prompt-panel-runner.ts + evidence.ts.

The open seat, precisely: "$29-79/mo flat, all engines, unlimited team, agency multi-brand, real free tier, no-signup grader, actions with receipts, GA4 attribution, white-label at sub-$100" — a combination no competitor offers, and every single element is validated by a winner already succeeding with it.
