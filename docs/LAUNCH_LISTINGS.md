# Launch listings — G2 & Product Hunt (Master Plan v4 Phase 4)

These are external actions only the founder can complete (both platforms require
a human account owner). Everything the listings need from the product is live.

## Product Hunt

1. Create a maker account at producthunt.com and claim the PresenceOS product page.
2. Assets:
   - Tagline (60 chars): "AI visibility tracking with verifiable receipts"
   - Description: lead with the differentiators — every feature on every plan
     ($29–$199 cap), hash-chained receipts anyone can verify at `/verify/{id}`,
     multi-run panels with confidence intervals, weekly action sprints with
     honest before/after verdicts.
   - Gallery: screenshot the live pages — `/pricing` (calculator), `/audit`
     result with competitor reveal, a `/verify` receipt page, the sprint panel.
   - First comment: the honesty angle (deleted our own fabricated case studies;
     verdicts can say "unchanged"; thin samples say "inconclusive").
3. Launch day: reply to every comment; the free no-signup grader at `/audit` is
   the conversion path — link it, not the signup page.

## G2

1. Create a vendor profile (g2.com/products/new) — category: "AI Visibility /
   Generative Engine Optimization" (also list under SEO tools).
2. Profile requirements: logo, 4+ screenshots, pricing (public: $0 / $29 / $79 /
   $199 — mark "all features on every plan"), and the `/pricing` URL.
3. Reviews: G2 requires genuine users. Source them from pilot agencies after
   their first completed sprint cycle (they'll have real before/after numbers
   to talk about). Never incentivize wording; incentivizing an honest review
   with a gift card is allowed under G2 rules if disclosed.
4. Comparison traffic: G2 auto-generates "PresenceOS vs X" pages once listed —
   these reinforce the on-site `/vs/*` pages.
